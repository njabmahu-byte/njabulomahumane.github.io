Strategic Rationale

Why This Calendar
Works.

April — Awareness
Trust Before Transaction

April is intentionally zero pressure. The audience encounters AETHER through the lens of values — sustainability proof, ingredient transparency, and Earth Day authenticity — before they're ever asked to buy. The Welcome Series email sequence ends with a soft first-purchase nudge only after the brand has established credibility across three touchpoints. The 10% welcome discount is offered once, quietly, never repeated. This mirrors how premium brands earn loyalty: by being worth knowing before being worth buying.

May — Engagement
Authority Through Education

May shifts the audience from aware to invested. The TikTok myth-debunking series builds authority without arrogance. The skin type carousel personalises the brand at scale. The male skincare content deliberately expands the demographic without alienating the core female audience — it reframes rather than pivots. The Nordic Night teaser at the end of the month is designed to plant a seed, not make a sale. The black-background email is the first visual departure from the brand norm — a deliberate signal that something different is coming.

June — Conversion
Scarcity That Means Something

The Nordic Night launch works because it was earned, not announced. Two months of trust-building mean the 500-unit limitation feels real, not manufactured. The dark visual language of June — navy, aurora tones, volcanic stone — creates a visual event that stands apart from the brand's usual aesthetic. The units counter story mechanic creates daily micro-urgency without a single "BUY NOW" message. The quarter closes with gratitude and Q3 anticipation — retaining the audience that took the hardest action: purchasing.

Platform Strategy
Instagram
Brand Home

Primary visual identity platform. Carousel format dominates for save-rate and educational depth. Reels drive discovery. Stories run conversion mechanics (polls, countdowns, waitlist).

TikTok
Discovery Engine

Acquisition-first. Myth debunking and routine demos designed for algorithmic spread. Lower brand polish, higher authenticity. Comments mined for community insight.

LinkedIn
Authority Builder

Targets brand builders, wellness industry professionals, and corporate buyers. Thought leadership positions AETHER as a business model, not just a product.

Email
Relationship Core

The highest-value channel. The 3-part welcome series, teaser mechanics, and launch drops happen here first. Email audience gets priority access as a reward for trust.

Blog / Website
SEO Foundation

Long-form content captures organic search traffic. Articles target high-value terms ('clean skincare', 'minimalist routine', 'men's skincare'). Internal links drive product page traffic.
