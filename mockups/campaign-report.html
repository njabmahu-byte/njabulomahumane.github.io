01 — Executive Summary

A Quarter That Moved the Needle

Q1 2026 exceeded engagement targets by 18% and drove the first measurable improvement in trust metrics after 12 months of decline. Trustpilot moved from 2.1 to 2.3 — the first upward movement in 18 months. Average response time improved 51%, dropping from 45 minutes to 22.

Quarter Headline Finding

"Behind-the-Scenes content generated +34% higher positive sentiment than any other pillar and directly correlated with the first Trustpilot improvement in 18 months. Transparency is not a soft brand value — it is a measurable trust lever with quantifiable commercial impact."

🏆
Top 3 Wins
Reels drove 73% of total Instagram engagement at only 40% of posting volume — 2.3x outperformance versus all other formats
Behind-the-Scenes pillar generated +34% positive sentiment above average and directly correlated with Trustpilot improvement
#MyTravelAlterEgo UGC reached 8,200 posts — 64% above the 5,000 target
📋
Critical Learnings
AI Trip Planner demos underperformed at 0.9% engagement vs 2.4% average — requires tutorial-first pivot in Q2
TikTok room tour format exceeded expectations at 5.3% engagement, validating discovery platform role
Response time improvement generated +28% positive sentiment on X support interactions

08 — Learnings & Recommendations

Scale, Iterate, Cut

Campaign Timeline — Key Events
January · Week 1
Campaign Launch — "Book Whoever You Want to Be"
All platforms activated simultaneously. #MyTravelAlterEgo UGC challenge launched. Tina Fey campaign live.
January · Week 3
Behind-the-Scenes Pillar + Rapid Response Protocol
Customer service spotlights began. Response time dropped from 45min to sub-30min within 7 days of protocol launch.
February
Viral Complaint Case Study + Carousel Engagement Dip
18-minute viral complaint resolution became internal template. Carousel ER dropped to 0.9% — attributed to format fatigue.
March
Carousel Recovery · YouTube Series Launch · UGC Target Exceeded
"Meet the Mixes" carousel (2.8% ER). YouTube "Master Perfumer" educational series launched. UGC hit 8,200 — 64% above target.
What Worked — Scale These
FindingRecommended Action
Reels drive 73% of Instagram engagementIncrease Reels to 50% of Instagram content mix. Redirect long-form video budget to Reels production.
Room tour format on TikTok delivers 5.3% ERLaunch weekly "Room Tour Tuesday" series. Partner with 5 new travel creators aligned to this format.
Behind-the-Scenes content builds measurable trustMonthly "Meet the Team" series. Expand to "Day in the Life" format. Never reduce this pillar's volume.
Rapid response improved sentiment 28%Reduce response time target to 20 minutes. Expand community team hours to cover weekend peaks.
What to Iterate — Refine These
ProblemRecommended Iteration
AI Trip Planner demos at 0.9% (vs 2.4% avg)Pivot to tutorial format: "How to plan a trip in 60 seconds." Use real user examples, not product demos.
Local Guides underperforming at 1.9% ERShift from "top 10" to "insider secrets" angle. Partner with local creators who have genuine authority.
Carousel engagement declined in FebruaryA/B test 3-slide micro-carousels vs 7-slide deep dives. Test headline formats for Slide 1.
What to Stop — Cut These
FormatRationale
Long-form video (3–5 min) on Instagram28 eng/$ vs Reels at 190/$. The cost-efficiency gap cannot be justified at this stage of the strategy.
Generic "destination inspiration" static posts1.1% engagement. Every dollar here produces 45x less engagement than Stories. Reallocate immediately.
Facebook boosted posts without videoVideo posts drive 3.2x higher ROI than static on Facebook. No exceptions to this rule in Q2.
