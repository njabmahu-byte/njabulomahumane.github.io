YOUR 14-DAY SKILL LAUNCH PLAN
A concrete structure for your next skill. Use it, adapt it, break it where it doesn't fit.
1
Days 1 — 3
Explore & Scope
Goal: understand the landscape and define your first project
  • Find the 20% of the skill that gives 80% of results — ask ChatGPT: "What are the core concepts to start with [skill]?"
  • Watch or read 2–3 high-quality overviews. No more.
  • Define one specific thing you'll build by Day 7
  • Create your one-page summary of key concepts
2
Days 4 — 7
Build Something Real
Goal: create your first output, regardless of quality
  • Open the tool. No tutorials running in the background.
  • Build the thing you defined. When stuck, search for that specific problem — not a general tutorial.
  • Finish it. Even if it's bad. Especially if it's bad.
  • Show it to someone — peer, mentor, or internet stranger
3
Days 8 — 14
Teach Someone Else
Goal: lock in learning through explanation
  • Find one person to walk through it with — a colleague, friend, or online community
  • Prepare a 10-minute explanation of what you built and learned
  • Answer their questions — their confusion is your gap map
  • Update your one-page summary with what you missed
Bad work today enables good work tomorrow.
