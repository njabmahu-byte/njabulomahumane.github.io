Content Strategy Framework
7 Fundamentals of
Good Content Writing
A blueprint for content that converts.
1
Precise Topic Selection
Narrow your focus. One clear topic, one clear angle. Broad topics confuse — specific topics convert.
Stay specific No vague angles
2
Target Your Prospect
Know exactly who you're writing for before you write a single word. Speak to everyone and you reach no one.
Ideal customer Demographics Pain points
3
Determine the Objective
Every piece of content must serve a clear goal — or it shouldn't exist.
+ Sales + Brand Awareness Added Value Entertainment
4
Educate the Reader
Teach something real. If your reader finishes and knows more than when they started, you've done your job. Information earns trust.
Build trust Practical insight
5
Deliver Value
Ask: "Would I save this?" If not, rewrite it. Content that adds no value gets ignored, forgotten, or worse — skipped before it's even finished.
Save-worthy Share-worthy
6
Set the Specs
Understand who would actually buy what you're promoting. Match your format, length, and tone to that specific buyer — not a general audience.
Buyer profile Format match Channel fit
7
Generate Action / Change Belief
Every piece needs a clear next step. If you don't tell your reader what to do, they'll do nothing. Make it obvious, make it easy.
