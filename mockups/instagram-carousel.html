Strategic Rationale

Target Audience

SA students and junior professionals aged 20–28 looking to break into or advance in digital marketing. Platform: Instagram Reels/Carousels.

Hook Strategy

Slide 1 uses a "save this" prompt and a specific promise — "5 skills that'll get you hired." Specificity drives saves, which signals high-quality content to the algorithm.

Value Architecture

Each slide delivers one complete idea. No padding. Progressive revelation keeps users swiping, and the final CTA payoff rewards their attention.

Visual System

Dark editorial colour palette. Amber and blue accents for hierarchy. Consistent slide numbering builds trust and completion. Brand signature on final slide only — not distracting early.
