Audio Mood

Starts silent (1s) for pattern interrupt. Upbeat lo-fi hip-hop beat drops on scene 2 and carries through to end. Keep VO loud — 70% of TikTok views are sound-on.

Text Overlays

Bold white text, black drop shadow, top-centre placement. Use TikTok's built-in text with bounce animation for each scene cut.

Editing Rhythm

Hard cuts — no transitions. Keep it snappy. Scene 1–4 at ~1 cut/second pace. Scenes 5–6 slower to let the pivot land. Total: 25 seconds max.

Creator Brief

Energetic but relatable. No corporate tone. Speak like you're texting a friend. Facial expressions carry the message — recruiters see a LOT of stiff talking heads.

Audio Mood

Starts silent (pattern interrupt). Then an aspirational, upbeat trending sound enters — something like a cinematic piano or popular lo-fi trending audio. Match cuts to the beat where possible.

Visual Style

POV style mixed with stat cards and text-only frames. Avoid stock photography — it reads as inauthentic on TikTok. Real graduate photos or creator-filmed content only.

Comment CTA Strategy

Asking users to comment "READY" serves dual purpose: it boosts engagement signals to the algorithm, and it creates a DM list the brand can follow up with directly.

Brand Placement

Brand mention intentionally held until frame 5 (75% through). The watch time retention signal is better when you deliver value before the reveal — it feels earned, not like an ad.
